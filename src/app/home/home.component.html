About Us

Launched amidst the COVID-19 outbreak, Expoed provides a platform for restaurants to seek immediate financial support from diners. In the form of donations or rewards-based Expos, diners can choose to support their favorite restaurants in a time of dire need. Businesses within the restaurant industry have been materially impacted by this shut-down, many of which are at risk of closing permanently as a result of the outbreak. At Expoed, we aim to better connect restaurants in need with those who are willing and able to support.
